WEB ACCESSIBILITY TOOLS

There are numerous testing tools that you can use to evaluate the accessibility of your website.

1. WAVE

By putting in your site URL, WAVE provides you with a visual representation of the portions of your website that could use improvement. WAVE will highlight the sections of your website that fail to meet the WCAG standards

2. SortSite

SortSite reveals any issues with color contrast on your webiste. You can enter your website URL or specific colors to test the contrast. If an error is detected, the site will provide recommnedations according to WCAG standards.

3. Luma

Luma helps to identify content that may contain flashing lights and other seizure-causing activites. Using precise frame-by-frame analysis, seizure prevention technology, and compliance with industry standards it ensures that your website is safe for all users.
